    Engineering For The Other 90%

Who We Are

    Project90 is an engineering club at the University of Calgary that runs hands-on design projects and partners with local non-profits to help communities in need.

Purpose

    - We provide the opportunity to apply academic knowledge in building practical design projects.
    - Whenever we notice the need for help, we rush right in and help to do our best.

Goals

    - Working on projects of different scales, we aim to serve communities in need.
    - Whether it is on university campus or outside, we engage in activities to develop real world skills.

Plans

    - As our vision expands, we look for new projects to part take in and events to host.
    - Knowledge is valuable and every chance to learn something new intrigues us.
